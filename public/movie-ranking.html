The Best Movies According to Becky

My top 3 movies of all time

Pitch Perfect

Combines a great deal of comedy, sarcasm, and wit. The music is great and continually keeps me entertained.

The Other Woman

Hilariously entertaining, great actors, and a good story line.

Liar Liar

Classic, hilarious, and heartwarming. Jim Carrey did an A+ job in his role.
